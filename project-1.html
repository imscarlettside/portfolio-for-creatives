The Sinful Squash Wine

Packaging /// Copywriting

Vegan restaurants are almost exclusively focused on leafy salad bowls and low-carb options, but what about the vegans that are looking for hearty, rich comfort food without compromising on their plant-based diet?

The Sinful Squash Wine Series is the house wine of a fictitious vegan comfort food restaurant. The brand is centred around leaning into the feeling of guilty indulgence, and the wine series expands on this brand tone. The Sinful Squash's signature wine was made for all who want to shed their petty shames and give rein to their unholy desires, all while keeping it plant-based. In order to tempt our target market into embracing the impure, we decided to draw upon the most direct form of degeneracy—the seven deadly sins. Each wine represents one sin, and our perfectly curated flavours were chosen specifically to encapsulate the taste of that sin.

The feeling

Take our Syrah Rosé, Envy; can you remember a time when you were seething with jealousy? The Sinful Squash took that bitter sting in your mouth and bottled it, because even the most depraved emotions deserve to be embraced without regret. Each sin is represented by a baroque painting luxuriously layered over spot UV gloss inspired by the designs of Roman Catholic windows.
