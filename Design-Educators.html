2025 RGD Design Educators

Social Media /// Print /// Real World

In spring 2025, I had the opportunity to design RGD’s Vancouver Design Educator’s event. I developed conceptual ideas and was responsible for designing all collateral for print and digital. I decided to focus this year’s event on the concept of diffusion by design. Through the representation of halftone gradients, the event’s visual language communicates how the spread of ideas and knowledge fosters growth in the industry of design education.
