FEA: Foundation of Expressive Art

Branding /// Packaging /// Motion

Many people could benefit from expressive art therapy, a flexible creative approach to healing, but do not access it due to misconceptions that it requires artistic skill or due to practical barriers like cost and scheduling. To solve this, I developed Fea, a foundation that reframes the practice as accessible and grounded in judgment free play. The visual language supports this with minimalist design and crayon strokes to balance professionalism with approachability. Clear, empathetic messaging demystifies the therapy and guides people toward resources that fit their lives.
