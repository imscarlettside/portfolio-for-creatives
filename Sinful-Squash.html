The Sinful Squash Wine

Packaging /// Copywriting

Vegan restaurants are almost exclusively focused on leafy salad bowls and low-carb options, but what about the vegans that are looking for hearty, rich comfort food without compromising on their plant-based diet? The Sinful Squash Wine Series is the house wine of a fictitious vegan comfort food restaurant that indulges your guilty pleasures.. This series playfully embodies the seven deadly sins, with each wine representing a different vice.

The feeling

Take our Syrah Rosé, Envy; can you remember a time when you were seething with jealousy? The Sinful Squash took that bitter sting in your mouth and bottled it. Each sin is represented by a baroque painting luxuriously layered over spot UV gloss inspired by the designs of Roman Catholic church windows.

Neck Label

Once bought, the paper on the neck can be unfurled to reveal the story behind the painting and how it ties into the wines sin, such as Cain’s jealousy-fuelled murder of his brother, Abel.
