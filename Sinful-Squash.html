The Sinful Squash Wine

Packaging /// Copywriting

Vegan restaurants are almost exclusively focused on leafy salad bowls and low-carb options, but what about the vegans that are looking for hearty, rich comfort food without compromising on their plant-based diet? The Sinful Squash Wine Series is the house wine of a fictitious vegan comfort food restaurant that indulges your guilty pleasures. This series playfully embodies the seven deadly sins, with each wine representing a different vice.
